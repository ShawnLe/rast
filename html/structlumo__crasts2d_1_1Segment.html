check whether point a & b are within two boundaries of the segment

Returns
uq or lq = qa * qb * ((high - low) + 2 * delta)
where:
qa = max(0.0, 1.0 - sqr(erra) / sqr(eps))
qb = max(0.0, 1.0 - sqr(errb) / sqr(eps))
